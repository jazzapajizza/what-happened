ABOUT US

CheerAngels is founded by Helen Ding, an Official Zumba Instructor, a passionate runner, and a lifelong dance enthusiast.

Over the past few years, she has led the team to cheer at major marathon events across Toronto, bringing energy and encouragement through music and movement.

Helen insists on high-quality performance standards and values the true spirit of marathon running. She volunteers her time to lead the team and believes in cheering with real love and passion.

CheerAngels began as a small team in 2020. Five years later, we have grown into a powerful and united community of spirited volunteers. In 2025 we were crowned Champion Cheer Station at the 2025 TCS Toronto Waterfront Marathon.
